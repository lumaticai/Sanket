# Sanket - Serverless Sign Language Translastor
[_The Voice for the Unheard_]()

![231389297-ec106b52-0072-4804-af49-84357594dd78](https://github.com/lumaticai/Sanket/assets/91174719/4bf5907e-d26f-48aa-8a5e-9e39f71d7c41)

### [Try Now: https://lumaticai.github.io/Sanket/](https://lumaticai.github.io/Sanket/)


Creating a sign language tool for deaf and speech-impaired individuals helps them communicate better with others. This tool works on any device and even without internet. It translates signs in real-time and can also turn text into signs. With lots of signs to choose from and options to adjust, it makes communication easier. Plus, it helps users learn sign language and connect with others in their community. Using the latest technology and unique innovation our solution aims to solve the age old issue of language inclusivity with the simplest and most accessable ways.

## Things that make the difference!
**#[ NO SERVERS REQUIRED! ]**

✅ Day to day life communication with a realtime-crossplatform offline capable webapp

✅ Sign Language Font (UNIQUE of it kind) a font made using sign language guestures images that can be embeded in websites and softwares for simless experience

✅ AI-Memory Feature (RAG-Google Gemini) the AI automatically responds and suggests replys based on the user queries (quality of life improvement)

✅ Active Learning Page - enables people to learn sign language even if it is not affordable to them.


## Some Features
✨ sign to text/voice

✨ voice/text to sign

## Challenges we ran into and conqured! ✅
This was an amazing project to do but we are proud some of the chalanges we successfully overcomed during this hackathon:

➡️ Implementing TensorFlow models on web technologies presented a significant challenge due to the need to optimize performance and ensure compatibility across various web browsers and devices.

➡️ There was a shortage of comprehensive sign language datasets beyond American Sign Language (ASL), which hindered the effective training and fine-tuning of our models for other sign languages.

➡️ Creating a modular font for Indian Sign Language (ISL) while ensuring scalability for adaptation to other sign languages posed a complex design challenge.

➡️ Overcoming these obstacles required extensive research, collaboration within the sign language community, and meticulous attention to detail in both technical and design aspects.

➡️Despite the challenges, our team remained committed to delivering a robust and inclusive sign language solution, leveraging creativity, perseverance, and collaboration to overcome each hurdle encountered.
